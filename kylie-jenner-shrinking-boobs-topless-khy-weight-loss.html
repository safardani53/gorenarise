Kylie Jenner reveals her shrinking boobs as she goes topless in Khy campaign after sparking concern

 Posted on June 14, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  503 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

KYLIE Jenner has flaunted her changed figure in a sultry new campaign video.

The Hulu star posed for her new brand, Khy in a video posted to Instagram on Monday.

In the post, Kylie, 26, modeled some new, more relaxed pieces from her new range.

While her brand has previously shown off skintight faux leather garments, Kylie switched things up in casual sweatpants.

The reality television personality wore her hair in a sleek updo, and accentuated her features with flawless, pouty makeup.

Showing off the oversized pieces, in some shots Kylie wore the hoodie unzipped, drowning her small frame while showing off her skin beneath.

In others, she revealed her weight loss by tying the hoodie over her boobs like a bra.

Kylie's arms looked very thin, while her defined shoulder blades were on display.

The skincare and cosmetics mogul revealed her completely flat stomach, and her chest looked far smaller than it used to be.

Captioning the social media post, the brand wrote: "The khy fleece oversized zip hoodie and fleece straight leg pant in light heather grey. coming 12/07 on khy.com."

In the comment section, one fan of the Kardashians noted Kylie's weight loss, and wrote: "She’s so beautiful! I need her ozempic!"

IS KYLIE OKAY?

While Kylie proudly shows off her body, some critics have debated whether her weight loss had gone too far.

When Kylie accepted her Wall Street Journal Magazine Innovator Award, some people noticed that her frame seemed slimmer than ever.

On Reddit, one critic said: "A little extra healthy weight makes people look a lot more youthful than if they were skinny, droopy and gaunt. Being very thin ages the crap out of people, and they verge on looking unwell. It’s not sexy or attractive."

A second agreed: "Rapid weight loss just makes one look sick. She’s pushing mid 40s before 30s."

Another accused: "She does not look like she is into the hard work of any sort like going to a gym. So yeah, definitely Ozempic and plastic surgery."

SECRET SURGERY?

Even prior to her recent look, Kylie's Oscars appearance earlier this year also sparked concern.

On a forum debating the mother of two's figure, one person stated: "That’s weight loss- and a lot of it."

A second person agreed, adding: "She looks like she lost 30 lbs in a few weeks."

One fan considered: "She lost weight and probably had some fat taken out as per the new thin trend."

The commenter above isn't the only person to suggest that Kylie's weight loss might not be fully natural.

After Kylie shared a TikTok video of her morning routine, people couldn't help but comment on Kylie's slimmed-down body.

However, many didn't believe that working out was the secret behind her slender figure.
