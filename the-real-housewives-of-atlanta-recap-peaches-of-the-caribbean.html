Kenya Moore Says Kim Fields' Husband is Gay

 Posted on October 3, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  278 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Sunday on The Real Housewives of Atlanta Season 8 Episode 14, the drama mounted between dueling s–t talkers Cynthia and Kenya.

Then Kenya asserted that Kim’s husband is gay. Seriously.

Watch The Real Housewives of Atlanta Season 8 Episode 14 Online

If you watch The Real Housewives of Atlanta online, you will see what could have been a run-of-the-mill feud escalate beyond belief.

When Kenya brought up the supposed rumors about Chris – Kim Fields’ husband – and his sexuality, that was, well, pretty abrasive.

"I know y’all have heard the rumors. They call him Chrissy," Kenya said after Kim went to prep for her commercial shoot in the morning.

"Who calls him that?" Phaedra Parks asked, noting that he can be "high strung and sassy" but skeptical that Kenya knows anything. 

"The industry," Moore shot back, channeling her inner tabloid. "For 10 years, there have been pervasive rumors about Kim’s husband."

The women laughed, noting Chris’ past career as a Broadway singer and dancer, as if he wouldn’t be openly gay if he were … gay.

Sheree Whitfield and Cynthia Bailey, surprisingly, stayed quiet despite having ample opportunities to knock Kenya down a peg (or 20).

The next night, Sheree wasn’t as willing to let it slide when the topic came up … and straight up told Fields what people were saying.

"The conversation about Chris is much different that last night," she told the Bravo cameras. "I can’t deal with it. I am allergic to fake s–t."

Naturally, we’re left hanging until next week as far as how the ladies react when held accountable for spreading this particular gossip.
