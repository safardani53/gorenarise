JEREMY Kyle pulled out all the stops as he finally wed his fiancee Victoria over the weekend.

The couple, who were forced to postpone their ceremony six times, said I Do at All Saints Church in Windsor, Berks in front of their nearest and dearest in a stunning pink themed wedding.

The stunning venue was decked out in the delightful hue, with large pink drapes adorning the wedding reception room, which matched the bridesmaid dresses.

Guests sat at circle tables with pink napkins and stunning floral arrangements.

The TV star, 56, and Vicky, 38,  treated their wedding party to a gift from Jo Malone each, before they tucked into a sumptuous three course meal.

After the wedding breakfast, guests headed to the dance floor to party the night away.

Bodyguard Big Steve, who rose to fame on The Jeremy Kyle Show, was amongst those in attendance and he was a huge hit with everyone there.

An insider said: “Jeremy and Vicky threw an epic wedding party.  The food was delicious, the wine flowed and everyone was in the mood to celebrate.

“Everyone wanted selfies with Big Steve, who happily posed away. There was a lot of love in the room.”

Speaking about his big day, Jeremy told The Sun: “Vick’s been my rock in my darkest times.

“So to see her today, having such fun enjoying the wedding of her dreams, was perfect.

“Thankfully, at what is our sixth attempt after all the Covid cancellations and postponements, we got to enjoy the big day we’d both longed for.”
