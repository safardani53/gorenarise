ASDA is claiming its new anti-ageing range delivers results within five minutes - and they're so confident they’ll give women their money back if they don’t agree.

Launching last week, the First Signs of Ageing contains a trio of products, priced from £6 to £10.

Asda has added to its NSPA beauty range with a daily perfecting serum, eye cream and complexion perfecting cream.

The vegan formulas claim to show results in just five minutes – and will refund anyone who doesn’t agree.

They said: “The nspa Expert First Signs of Ageing collection has been specifically created by Spa Experts to support skin and ensure it looks beautifully smooth and insta-skin ready.

“Our clinically proven Daily Complexion Perfecting Cream doesn't take weeks for a result, oh no, see a difference in minutes.

“User tests show that fine lines and wrinkles are plumped and visibly smoother in just 30 minutes.

“Tests show significant visible reduction in crow's feet wrinkles in just 5 minutes.”

They added: “Nspa are so confident in the powerful skin-boosting powers of the First Signs of Ageing range, that they promise to reveal noticeably smoother and more radiant skin in minutes... or your money back.”

The products, which launched on Saturday, are available in-store and online.
