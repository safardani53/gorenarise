The Curse of Bridge Hollow Is Set To Be Released On Netflix Soon

 Posted on April 1, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  444 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

The Curse of Bridge Hollow, Netflix’s first adventure-horror comedy, is set to premiere on the platform on Friday, October 14, 2022, at 12 a.m. PT. The film follows a father-daughter team who are determined to safeguard their community after an evil ghost creates havoc by bringing eerie Halloween decorations to life. The film’s principal actors are Marlon Wayans and Priah Ferguson.

The Curse of Bridge Hollow on Netflix will undoubtedly be frightening.

On September 14, 2022, Netflix released the official trailer for the film, providing a glimpse of the film’s eerie universe. The trailer doesn’t spend any time in establishing the primary premise in the first minute. The clip has a number of humorously chaotic scenes and promises a campy and enjoyable encounter.

Overall, the trailer has a strange and humorous tone that horror-comedy aficionados will certainly like. Along with the trailer, Netflix released a synopsis of the film, which reads:

“After an old and mischievous ghost forces Halloween decorations to come to life and wreak havoc, a father (Marlon Wayans) and his teenage daughter (Priah Ferguson) are compelled to join up and rescue their town.”

One of the most noticeable parts of the trailer is how much time it spends building the characters and their interactions with one another without giving away too much critical plot information that may damage the watching experience. According to the narrative and teaser, audiences may anticipate an irreverent horror-comedy in the vein of The Witches and Army of Darkness.

More information about the actors and director of The Curse of Bridge Hollow

Howard is played by Marlon Wayans in The Curse of Bridge Hollow. Wayans looks great in the trailer, and his distinct humorous charm dominates the film. Viewers can expect an enjoyable performance from this brilliant actor-comedian. Apart from The Curse of Bridge Hollow, Marlon Wayans is well-known for his roles in a number of notable films and television programs, including The Wayans Bros, Requiem for a Dream, and Fifty Shades of Grey, to mention a few.

Along with Wayans in the major role, teenage actress Priah Ferguson plays Sydney, who looks as remarkable in the trailer, effortlessly capturing the tension and eccentricity of her character. Ferguson will be recognized by Stranger Things fans as Erica Sinclair. Her other television and film credits include The Oath, Atlanta, Bluff City Law, and a slew of others.

Jeff Wadlow directed the film, which was written by Todd Berger and Robert Rugan. Wadlow is a well-known film director best recognized for his work on films such as Fantasy Island, Never Back Down, and Cry, Wolf. Remember to check out The Curse of Bridge Hollow on Netflix on Friday, October 14, 2022.
