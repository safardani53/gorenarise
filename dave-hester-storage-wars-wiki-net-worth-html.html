2. Dave Hester's Blooming Net Worth

Being one of the main cast of the reality series Storage Wars, Dave Hester has garnered some appetizing wealth till date.

4. Dave Hester & His On-screen Time On Storage Wars

Being known from the series Storage Wars, Dave Hester has not failed to make himself known. Dave debuted in the series in 2010, and while the show had a lousy start, it soon gained popularity in its second season.

Known for his appalling and competitive personality, Dave was involved in many conflicts and tussle during the series. When his brother appeared on the second season of Storage Wars, the siblings had a feud, and Dave placed bid against him.

Dave was banned from the series in 2012, to which he filed a lawsuit against A&E for wrongful termination. In July 2014, Dave settled with A&E Network, and the terms have been sealed. He has returned to the show as well. 

As of now, he lives up to the nickname he got from the show, The Mogul, following his opulence and resides in a single-family home in Westminster.
