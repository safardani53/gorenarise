MUCH-LOVED radio DJ and TV personality Jackie O has made a very successful career for herself since first appearing on the radio back in the early 90's.

Since then the 48-year-old has formed a great on air relationship with fellow host Kyle Sandilands and earned herself an eye-watering net worth during her career.

What is Jackie O’s real name?

Popular Australian Radio host Jacqueline Ellen Last is better known by her nickname Jackie O.

This unique name was given to her back in the early 90's when she first started her radio presenting career alongside Phil O'Neil as the pair co-hosted Triple M Adelaide.

The reason for her nickname hasn't ever been properly explained by the star but many have praised Jackie for the memorable and sweet nickname.

She is also often referred to as Jackie 'O' Henderson because of her former relationship with her ex-husband Lee Henderson.

What is Jackie O's net worth?

After years of building up her career as a successful radio and TV host, Jackie O has earned herself an impressive net worth.

According to accumulate.com, the Aussie celeb is worth around $5million, as of 2023.

How long has Jackie O presented with Kyle?

Kyle Sandilands and Jackie O first met when they were both young and up comers on the Today Network.

After hitting it off and finding a lot of success the lovable pair were moved from the drive-time show to prime time breakfast show in the early 2000s.

For years the dynamic duo lead the Hot Hits countdown segment which later saw them branch off into The Kyle And Jackie O Show that is now one of Australia's most popular shows.

Jackie and Kyle were also the co-hosts of Big Brother Australia for a brief period in a huge career move for the duo into TV.

Is Jackie O married?

Jackie O is currently single but has had two previous marriages.

The host split from her Brit photographer husband Lee Henderson in 2018 after being together for 15 years.

Jackie has a 12-year-old daughter Kitty with Lee who the pair share custody over.

Her first marriage was to her then radio co-host Phil O'Neil in 1994 - when Jackie was just 19-years-old.

The pair quickly sparked a romantic connection a year earlier and their relationship was the catalyst for Jackie's career in radio.

However their divorce came around after just a few years as O'Neil moved to the London and the pair moved on with their lives.
