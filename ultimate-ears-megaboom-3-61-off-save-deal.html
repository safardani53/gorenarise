Ultimate Ears Megaboom 3 is 61 cheaper than usual, but not for long

 Posted on January 1, 0001  |  3 minutes  |  475 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

AMAZON has dropped the price of Ultimate Ears’ Megaboom 3 speaker to just £108.

The new price is part of the online giant’s Deals of the Day, so you’ll need to be quick.

The new price-cut comes after an earlier sale this week by Amazon.

Discounts were available across Ultimate Ears Bluetooth speaker range, with savings of up to £44.

  • Ultimate Ears Megaboom 3, from £108, at Amazon - buy here

That specific saving was for the Megabom 3, although discounts could also be found on the brand’s other products - including the Wonderboom 2 Boom, Blast and Megablast.

But the new Megaboom 3 deal is now even cheaper, with up to £61 off of the Black model of the Bluetooth speaker.

Black models tend to be the most popular of Ultimate Ears’ speakers - it does go with everything - and so they can go out of stock when discounts arrive.

But if you do miss the Black, you can also get a top saving on the Blue model, where you’ll save £55.

Or, if you prefer to keep things muted, the Megaboom 3 Grey is a solid £50 off.

Regardless of colour, the savings are pretty hefty for a Bluetooth speaker which has some very decent sound and features.

The highly rated device is second only in power to its larger sibling, the Megablast, and still has plenty to like.

Firstly, it offers 360 degree sound, to ensure it can be heard on all sides if you're making it the centrepiece of a room for max coverage.

Combined with its 45m Bluetooth range, you’ll get good flexibility on where you want it situated.

Its 20-hour battery should be plenty to keep it running through barbecues and days in the sun - and it even supports wireless charging, though you’ll need to pick up a separate charger for that.

The Megaboom 3 is also waterproof enough to allow it to be submerged in water to to 1m for 30 minutes, which should help it survive splashes and summer downpours.
