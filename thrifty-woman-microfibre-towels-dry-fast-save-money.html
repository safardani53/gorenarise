WHEN it comes to drying laundry, towels are one item that seem to take days - or even weeks - to dry.

But one thrifty woman has revealed the long-term investment she's made to help.

The self-described 'Duchess of Thrift' took to her TikTok page to rave about the £23.99 microfibre towel bale she'd got from Amazon, as she called them a "miracle".

"Out of all of the tips that I've shared so far, this is probably going to be the most useful," she began.

"It solves the problem that I've had so many questions about, and that is how to dry your towels.

"So we're really struggling with the cost of our tumble drying, we're really struggling with the cost of our heating, we're really struggling to get things dry, and the worst thing to get dry are towels.
