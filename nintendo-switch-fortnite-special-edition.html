Nintendo Switch Fortnite Special Edition is available NOW - but only while stocks last

 Posted on September 30, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  372 words  |  Aldo Pusey

FORNITE fans, there’s a new bit of hardware to drop in on from the battle bus.

The Nintendo Switch Fortnite Special Edition has launched today.

Nintendo’s new Fortnite themed bundle is only available from limited stores, and while stocks last, so you’ll need to be quick.

At the time of writing the special edition it’s available directly from Amazon, Game, Nintendo and ShopTo - and comes with some neat touches including Fortnite pre-installed.

Whether it’s for an existing Fortnite fan, or anyone who wants a great looking Switch in their lives this Christmas, don’t hang around if you’re keen.

Priced at the standard £279.99 it's well priced, and you get some added extras to help you get started in Fortnite's battle royales.

The Nintendo Switch Fortnite Special Edition itself looks great – featuring Yellow / Blue Joycon controllers.

It also has a unique Fortnite design on the console dock, as well as on the back of the console.

Fortnite comes pre-installed on the Switch, but you’ll also get some free in-game treats to help you on your path to Battle Royale victory.

The Fortnite Special Edition includes a free Wildcat Bundle download code as well as 2,000 of Fortnite’s in-game V-Bucks currency - not to be sniffed at.

So should you be looking to pick up a Switch in this year’s Black Friday deals, this is a pretty decent offer to consider.

The Switch Fortnite Special Edition may feature in offers during November, but like most special editions, stock will dictate what offers are available.
