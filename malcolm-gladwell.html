Malcolm Gladwell Age, Biography

Malcolm Gladwell was born Malcolm Timothy Gladwell on September 3, 1963. His birthplace is Fareham, Hampshire, England. He is of English and Afro-Jamaican ancestry and is of Canadian nationality. He is the son of Graham Gladwell, an English father, and Joyce Gladwell, an Afro-Jamaican mother. His father teaches mathematics, and his mother is a therapist. In 2021, he celebrated his 58th birthday. He relocated to Ontario, Canada with his family when he was six years old and lived his entire childhood there. He has two older brothers.

Malcolm spent his upbringing in rural Ontario Mennonite country, attending a Mennonite church. His great-great-great-grandmother was Igbo from Nigeria, West Africa. He finished his studies at Ontario High School. In 2007, Gladwell received an honorary doctorate from the University of Waterloo after graduating from high school. Gladwell interned at the National Journalism Center in Washington, D.C. in the spring of 1982. In 1984, he earned a bachelor’s degree in history from Trinity College at the University of Toronto.
