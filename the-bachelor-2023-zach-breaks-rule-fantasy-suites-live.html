Fans think Zach and Kaity leaked a ‘clue’

Fans accused Zach and Kaity of leaking a “clue” about their engagement prior to The Bachelor’s final episode of the season after the pair shared similar posts on social media.

The 28-year-old Bachelor contestant recently shared a snap of herself posing with a huge barbecue rib on her Instagram Stories.

Someone appeared to take the pic of her since in the snap both of Kaity’s hands were on her rib as she held it up to her mouth.

The reality show competitor had a huge smile on her face as she modeled the rib, sporting a grey sweatshirt and ponytail, appearing to be ready for the messy meal.

Although Kaity didn’t caption her photo, she did tag the Texas-based restaurant, Terry Black’s Barbecue.

Anastasia accused of having boyfriend during filming

Cat Wong claimed in the Women Tell All special that Anastasia Keramidas was in a relationship when she filmed the show.

“Anastasia, you had a boyfriend on the show,” Cat said.

After Anastasia denied the claim, Cat responded, “Ok, boyfriend, you were f******, I don’t know.”

Anastasia was eliminated in the Bahamas when she was accused of participating in the competition to gain followers on social media.

No sex rule for Fantasy Suite bites Zach ‘in the a**’

Zach Shallcross made the decision not to have sex with his contestants Kaity Biggar, Ariel Frenkel, and Gabi Elnicki.

“From what I can say is — a lot of things do change and going into that week, I thought I could basically set up, like, a guideline for myself and a guideline for others to try to make it less messy and it bit us in the a**,” the 26-year-old Bachelor told Us Weekly at the Women Tell All taping.

Zach’s choice surprised host Jesse Palmer, who quipped that it might not be practical if he wants to find his significant other.

“Zach puts some parameters around fantasy suites that he thinks is going to help him make his decision at the end of the day,” he said.

“So Zach decides that he is not going to sleep with any of the three women, that he’s going focus on other means and ways of progressing his relationships, but things don’t exactly go that way.”

Ariel’s brother interrogated Zach

Once they enter a separate room, Bobby started in on Zach: “I don’t know you from Adam. I’m sure you’re a respectable guy. I’m sure you’re a nice guy. Whatever.

“But, uh, calling myself a skeptic and saying this is unorthodox is an understatement.”

He then asks Zach why his sister should choose him and agree to marry him.

Unfortunately for Zach, it didn’t seem like he had an answer. He struggled for a few seconds before blurting out, “I don’t know.”

Zach goes on to say: “It might sound simple, but I have a pretty big heart, and the person that I end up with at the end of this has my whole entire heart.”

He ends by making a joke and saying: “And, I’m a pretty good cook.”

Zach was ‘nervous’ before meeting Ariel’s family

Prior to the Bachelor meeting Ariel Frenkel’s family, she warned Zach that her brother Bobby is very protective and would be very tough on him.

Zach looked visibly nervous and admitted to being “very nervous” when the couple discussed meeting Ariel’s parents, but she was quick to warn him about Bobby.

The New Yorker said: “I don’t think my parents are the ones to worry about. I think it’s my brother. Bobby is so protective of me … He offered to beat up my exes.”

Before the Bachelor entered the winery, Bobby is caught on camera saying: “We’ll see how he does. I’m not holding my breath.”

Zach’s heart ‘fluttered’ after Charity’s confession

After Charity and Zach went line dancing in her hometown, the Georgia native told him that she was falling in love with him.

Zach said in a confessional that it felt “amazing” to hear.

“Hearing that from her makes my heart flutter and makes me really hopeful for what could be us at the end,” he said.

“The end” ended up coming soon as Zach sent Charity home during the rose ceremony.
