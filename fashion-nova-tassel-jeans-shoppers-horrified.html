SHOPPERS are recoiling in horror at a pair of jeans with full-length tassels attached to the front.

Fashion Nova is selling the bonkers trousers, which they said would “spice up your denim.”

Costing nearly £50, the light blue jeans have tassels sewn onto the front and along the pockets.

The shimmering tassels are only stitched to the front of the jeans - with a normal back.

The bizarre accessory has confused shoppers, who have questioned how you’d be able to complete daily tasks in the unusual jeans.

The brand shared a photo of thee outfit to their Instagram page, where it’s racked up more than 20,000 likes and nearly 200 comments.
