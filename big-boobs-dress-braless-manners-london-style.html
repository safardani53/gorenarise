A CURVY fashionista has sparked a frenzy for a figure-hugging midi dress that can be worn without a bra.

She wowed in the plunging brown dress as she shared a variety of different ways to style the wardrobe staple.

Posting under the handle @themothfaerie on TikTok, she spoke candidly with her 54,000 about the challenges she has experienced from having big boobs.

She looked confident as she donned the Saffi dress from Manners London in the color cafe brown, which has been reduced from $115 to $81.

The dress is sold in sizes ranging from XXS to XXXXL, and is available in a selection of other colors such as red, black, and pink.

She showed how it could be paired with a maxi skirt and mules for a relaxed look or teamed with a knitted top and knee-high boots in the fall.

She looked ready for summer as she wore the dress like a skirt with a Bardot top and crossbody bag. 

The trendsetter posed from different angles as she showed how versatile the dress was to style.

She penned a lengthy caption for the video as she called on all of her “big boobed faeries” to go braless.

She wrote: “@MannersLondon has kindly sent me this beautiful brown dress that is made to be worn WITHOUT A BRA!

“As a size G I of course was super skeptical at first but I am here to tell you: magic is REAL.

“This dress is so comfortable, so soft, and so beautiful. The two girls don’t go anywhere.”

She continued: “All jokes aside, the last time I was able to leave the house comfortable without a bra I was 11 years old.

“My back has endured too small bras, tight straps, crappy sports bras, and much more for over 10 years.

“And once I discovered this amazing brand I actually cried at the idea of being able to walk around freely without the pain.”

The fashionista revealed the dress wasn’t her first time shopping with the brand and that she’s worn the white tops countless times for work.

She added: “I cannot explain how important this is to me and I hope to you as well! And if not then share the news with one of the big-breasted people in your life!”

The video went viral and quickly garnered hundreds of likes from people who were impressed by her styling.

Many people took to the comment section complimenting her appearance.
