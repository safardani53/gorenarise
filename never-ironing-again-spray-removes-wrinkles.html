ARE you fed up with ironing and can’t be bothered to get rid of any wrinkles in clothes and sheets?

A woman has raved about a £2.50 spray from Asda that lets you spray away creases in seconds with little effort.

TikTok user @ellishjem showed how she used the Lenor Crease Releaser on a wrinkled shirt.

After spray over the garment, she then smoothed it out with her hand, and the creases fell out in seconds.

The product description says: “Lenor Spring Awakening Crease Releaser removes creases without the hassle of ironing.

“It eliminates odours too, leaving your clothes with a fresh, spring fragrance.”
