The Weeknd, whose real name is Abel Makkonen Tesfaye, and Selena dated for 10 months back in 2017.

In addition to the musician's high profile relationship with Selena, The Weeknd also dated model Bella Hadid, 24, from 2015-2016.

After their split and his relationship with Selena, The Weeknd and Bella reconciled as they dated again from 2018-2019.

The speculation that the song is about Selena comes one day after the Blinding Lights singer left his fans "scared" after debuted his freaky face from "plastic surgery" in the music video.

Before sporting the look in the video, The Weeknd showed up in another questionable look as he was covered in bandages at the American Music Awards months ago.

The singer was unmasked in the pop video, where his face had been altered with prosthetics that emulated major plastic surgery.

The Starboy hitmaker's freaky face appeared to have been pumped with fillers in his cheeks and lips, as well as his nose chiseled.

In the music video, a spotlight lit up his face, which was swollen from fake botox injections and made him appear sore around the pretend surgical scarring that mimicked a facelift.

While the strange face was completely fake, fans couldn't help but be shocked and "horrified" by the singer's changed look.
