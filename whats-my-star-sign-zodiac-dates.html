What are the characteristics of each star sign?

Each zodiac sign has different traits, strengths, and weaknesses, and each is represented by a symbol.

Aries ♈︎

Ruling planet: Mars

Element: Fire

Zodiac symbol: The Ram

Aries are natural-born leaders who are bold, and ambitious with a strong-minded nature.

They are symbolised by the ram and are famously passionate, fiery, and argumentative.

The "V" glyph represents the horns of the ram though it can be interpreted to show a fountain squirting a stream of water upwards.

Aries loves to be the centre of attention and are always on top of their competition and never miss out on a challenge.

Water represents a surge of youthful energy, a strong sense of identity, and consciousness bursting from Aries.

Aries can tie in with traits such as determination, high energy, and acting with conviction.

The sign is compatible with most others are people who are Aries tend to be easy-going, and friendly with a child-like personality making them to be around.

For relationships Aries are most compatible with their fellow Fire signs - Sagittarius and Leo, as well as air signs - especially Gemini.

Taurus ♉︎

Ruling planet: Venus

Element: Earth

Zodiac symbol: The Bull

Taurus signs are best known for their stubborn nature making the bull glyph perfect for this star sign.

The horns are said to represent extra sensory appendages of the bull, as Taureans are more in touch with their senses than any other sign in the zodiac.

Taurus is ruled by Venus which represents love, beauty, and money which means they are the most sensual sign of the zodiac.

If you're a Taurus you might be grounded in hard work, slow and steady perseverance, and security, the bull is the persistent provider of the zodiac.

They'll enjoy indulgences, luxuries, and physical pleasures and feel most content when pampered.

Taureans will also have steady, harmonious relationships as they are steadfast and loyal.

They'll be most compatible with other Earth signs - Virgo and Capricorn, or Water signs - Cancer, Pisces, and Scorpio.

Gemini ♊︎

Ruling planet: Mercury

Element: Air

Zodiac symbol: The Twins

Geminis are represented by the twin glyph, indicative of the two sides to those who fall under this Air sign.

This means Geminis can see two sides to a situation but it can also cause them to feel conflicted by opposing views.

Geminis usually go a hundred miles per hour, juggling hobbies, work, friendships, and romantic pursuits.

They tend to be passionate, playful, and intellectually curious.

Geminis are also intelligent, adaptable, and charming but possess nervous and indecisive traits.

Like dark and light, yin and yang, neither opposing side of the Gemini can exist without the other.

Their duality and adaptivity can sometimes be misrepresented as two-faced, but Geminis make loyal friends who will want to improve their relationships.

Gemini is ruled by Mercury, a planet representing communication meaning Geminis are talkative, expressive, and sociable.

Geminis' most compatible signs are Aries, Aquarius, Libra, and Leo.

Cancer ♋︎

Ruling planet: The Moon

Element: Water

Zodiac symbol: The Crab

If your star sign is Cancer you're most likely to be highly intuitive, emotional, and sensitive with a gentle and empathetic nature.

Cancer has two parallel symbols that come together to resemble a sideways  '69'.

The glyph is thought to represent a crab's claws or a woman's breast.

Cancerians don't let go of things easily once they have grabbed onto something such as an idea, goal or relationship.

Like the crab, Cancerians are guarded by a hard shell which they will need time and comfort to come out of.

They're homebodies and family-oriented and make great chefs and caregivers.

Cancers make a great match with people who people who understand and appreciate their emotional nature such as those whose star signs are Taurus, Virgo, Pisces, and Scorpio.

Leo ♌︎

Ruling planet: The Sun

Element: Fire

Zodiac symbol: The Lion

Leos are well-known for being bold, fierce, and wanting to be the centre of attention.

They are represented by the Lion and are often passionate, enthusiastic, and ambitious.

The glyph features a Lion with a circle representing the body and a swooping curved shape representing its mane and tail.

Leo is ruled by the sun meaning they're full of life and vitality and will never stop pursuing their many passions

Leos have inspiring friendship, and drama-fuelled romances but they'll run away as soon as they're bored.

The only signs that can keep up with them are other Fire signs, Sagittarius and Aries

Virgo ♍︎

Ruling planet: Mercury

Element: Earth

Zodiac symbol: The Virgin

Virgos are great with organization, and communication and have intelligence levels.

They're also logical, pragmatic, systematic and will work hard to achieve their goals.

Virgos have a perfectionist nature often coming across as critical and judgemental often being their own worst critics.

Virgos are ruled by the planet Mercury, the planet of communication, but unlike Geminis, Virgos process information by internalizing it and organizing it into clear concepts.

Centuries ago, Astrologists decided the glyph for Virgo represented an 'unspoiled' virginal woman.

The 'M' shape may stand for 'maiden' but others interpret the crossed loop to represent the closed legs of a chaste virgin.

Their best matches are Taurus, Cancer, and Pisces but they should avoid relationships with Gemini and Sagittarius.

Libra ♎︎

Ruling planet: Venus

Element: Air

Zodiac symbol: The Scales

Libra placements abstract glyph symbolizes their charm and charisma.

They're often easy to get on with and love to find a balance in all areas of their lives.

Libras need to be surrounded by beauty, symmetry, and art.

They're flirty by nature, find it difficult to commit to one partner, and are people-pleasers.

Libras are also indecisive and it can hold them back in life but they'll make up for it with their drive and ambition.

Libras are most compatible with Gemini, Taurus, Sagittarius, and Aquarius.

Scorpio ♏︎

Ruling planet: Pluto/Mars

Element: Water

Zodiac symbol: The Scorpion

Scorpios are a force to be reckoned with as they're often passionate, determined, and fierce.

It's said to be the most intense sign with Scorpios often being assertive, clever, cunning, and always a few steps ahead.

Scorpios also have a guarded, calculating nature that protects their intense inner emotional world.

This glyph looks similar to the letter 'M' with an arrow coming across from the right.

The shape is connected with the scorpion, with the arrow representing the insect's stinger.

When Scorpios fall in love, they fall hard but they tend to be picky about their relationships.

They get along best with their fellow water signs - Cancer, Pisces, and Scorpio.

Sagittarius ♐︎

Ruling planet: Jupiter

Element: Fire

Zodiac symbol: The Archer

Sagittarius are the travelers of the zodiac and often are adventurous, curious, intense, and adaptable.

Sagittarians have high-reaching ideals and often shoot for the stars when trying to live their lives in an idealistic fashion.

They're also known for being open-minded and intellectual meaning Sagittarians ponder philosophical questions such as the meaning of life.

Their glyph represents an arrow representing how Sagittarians aim for a goal or ambition and go after their dreams.

Sagittarius is ruled by Jupiter, the planet of abundance, Sagittarius is the luckiest sign and is never satisfied, always on the lookout for its next adventure.

Sagittarians value freedom and honesty meaning they find it hard to settle down in a relationship.

Their active, sociable energy needs to be matched meaning Sagittarius is most compatible with Aries and Leo.

Capricorn ♑︎

Ruling planet: Saturn

Element: Earth

Zodiac symbol: The Goat

In ancient mythology, Capricorn is actually represented by a sea goat, complete with fins and a tail.

The sea goat represents the dual nature of Capricorns.

They are hardworking, stubborn, and goal-oriented, typified by the goat 'half' of the glyph but they have an emotional side they conceal from others, represented by the sea goat's hidden fishtail.

Capricorns are very patient, materialistic, and determined, they don't stop until they get the job done.

In relationships, they'll appreciate someone who is loyal and consistent and can support them in their busy careers.

Capricorns best matches with are Taurus, Virgo, and Pisces.

They're usually the most financially stable of the zodiac signs.

Capricorns are ruled by Saturn which represents tasks, rules, and responsibilities.

Aquarius ♒︎

Ruling planet: Uranus

Element: Air

Zodiac symbol: The Water-Bearer

Aquarius are the humanitarians of the zodiac, aspiring to make the world a better place.

The water seen in Aquarius' glyph represents water pouring from the water bearer's pitcher in the constellation.

This signifies the Aquarian's innate aptitude for understanding the energy around them.

They can be stubborn and abrupt - and need their own space to think and develop ideas.

In a relationship, they are most compatible with Gemini, Libra, and Sagittarius, as they need someone clever and opinionated.

Those born in Aquarius's date range are assertive and analytical and can be often perceived as eccentric because of their unusual hobbies and fashion sense.

Uranus, the planet of innovation, technology, and surprising events, makes Aquarians great visionaries and agents of change.

Pisces ♓︎

Ruling planet: Neptune

Element: Water

Zodiac symbol: Two fish swimming in opposite directions

Pisces is the last constellation of the zodiac.

It represents those who are empathetic and compassionate.

Those who have the Pisces star sign can become overwhelmed with emotion, are great empaths, and feel everything deeply.

They're romantic, soulful, and intuitive but avoid their problems and confrontations.

Pisces will match well with those who are Scorpians, Cancerians, and Capricorns.

The glyph depicts two fish linked together moving in opposite directions.

It's meant to represent the way people under this sign feel connected to those around them.
