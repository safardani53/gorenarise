Former Boss: Real Housewives' Melissa Gorga Was Never a Stripper!

 Posted on June 17, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  419 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

On display? Not quite!

After Internet reports surfaced Friday that Real Housewives of New Jersey cast member Melissa Gorga was previously employed as a stripper, the man who once gave Gorga a gig — as a bartender! — is leaping to her defense.

Larry Guarino, Gorga's former manager at Lookers Gentleman's Club in Elizabeth, N.J., insists to Us Weekly reports of Gorga's past are grossly exaggerated, and that while the reality star did clock in to work at Lookers, it was not as a stripper.

"She was a bartender for a couple of months years ago, it was such a short time," Guarino tells Us of his former employee. "Lookers was really upscale then too; it wasn't like she was bartending in some hole in the wall."

Late Friday, Gorga herself took to her blog to slam the stripper rumors, declaring them "100% not true." Confirming she worked at Lookers during college, Gorga says her work outfit "was a tank top, shorts and sneakers. SORRY, no bubbies there," she wrote. "You will have a better chance at seeing some nudity on the [Housewives] episode this Sunday."

Rumors of Gorga's supposed profession spread earlier this week, when the Real Housewives cast came together to attend a fashion show at Posche, the boutique frequently seen on Bravo's series.

Though the Huffington Post reports that network producers brought Gorga's "former boss" to the show in order to confront her, Guarino insists the man, Angelo Voorhees, had little to no contact with her to begin with.
