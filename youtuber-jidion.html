JIDION has managed to earn more than seven million subscribers on his YouTube channel.

His funny prank and reaction videos are a hit with fans, but who is he?

Who is JiDion?

JiDion set himself up for a successful career in comedy after becoming known as the class clown in school.

He set up his YouTube account with his first uploads showing him playing games such as Call Of Duty and Grand Theft Auto.

His content style changed when he began to upload prank and reaction videos, which he is now known for.

JiDion was inspired by British YouTuber KSI when he was growing his channel and content.

The star is known for his cheeky side and his risky videos.

He was kicked out of the US Open in September 2022 after sitting in the front row and receiving a haircut during Nick Kyrgios' clash with Karen Khachanov.

However, he may have gone too far as he was handed a lifetime ban from Wimbledon after making gestures on camera during a quarter-final match between Novak Djokavic and Jannik Sinner.

This isn't his only ban as JiDion was permanently banned from streaming platform Twitch after encouraging his followers to "hate-raid" popular streamer Pokimane.

He then showed himself going to Twitch Con after being given the permanent ban.

Other pranks that JiDion has recorded include blowing an airhorn at a tennis match and trying to make the Queen's guards laugh.

What is JiDion's real name?

JiDion's real name is Jidon Adams.

He also goes by DeMarcus Cousins III after his grandfather, whose name is Demarcus Cousins I.

Is JiDion a part of Prime?

Yes, JiDion is a member of the Prime Squad.

The Prime Squad is made up of influencers, fronted by famous YouTubers Logan Paul and KSI.

The name came from the Prime Hydration sports drink which was created and launched by Logan and KSI.

Logan announced on June 21, 2022 that JiDion's was part of Prime Squad, where he said JiDion was the first to join the "group of up-and-comers".
