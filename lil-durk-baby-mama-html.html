Lil Durks Alleged Baby Mama x.meanass Blasts Him on Instagram

 Posted on July 22, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  502 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

It wasn’t long ago that Lil Durk expressed his enthusiasm over wanting a son with his fiancée India Royale.

On September 7, the rapper tweeted that he wanted another son, and it looks like his wish might come true. 

A woman with Instagram handle @x.meanass, is now alleging that the rapper was the father of her kids.

Later, x.meanass edited the post and noted that she made her post not because of she wanted an engagement and clarified that she also had no intentions of dating him.

She ended the post with “POSITIVE VIBES PLEASE.” 

x.meanass alleging Lil Durk is the father of her son reposted by Onsite (Source: Onsite Instagram)

x.meanass also tagged her son’s Instagram handle in the post. Meanwhile, Lil Durk has yet to respond to the woman’s claim. 

Lil Durk’s Kids and Baby Mama

If the claims end up being true, this would be Lil Durk’s seventh child. The rapper already has six kids with different baby mamas.

He had his first child when he was just 17 years old. The rapper had been dating Nicole Covone since 2008, and the two had their first child, Angelo Banks, in 2011.

After that, Covone gave birth to their second baby, Bella, in 2013.

Shortly after Bella was born, the rapper and Covone called it quits.

The rapper then became the father to his son, Zayden. The whereabouts of his baby mama are currently unknown.

Then, in 2014, the rapper announced that his son Du’mier Banks was born. 

But he did not reveal who Du’mier’s mother was. He is also the father of Skyler Banks, whose mother is believed to be Tameka Kute.

After Skyler Banks, the rapper fathered another daughter, Willow Banks, who was born in October 2018. 

The rapper shares Willow with his current flame, Royale.

The two started dating in 2017, and recently on December 20, Durk proposed to his baby mama during his concert in their hometown of Chicago. Naturally, Royale said yes, and the two were engaged. 

Royale explained that she wanted two more kids with Durk and shared that the rapper could not get enough of her when she was pregnant. 

Lil Durk and India Royale Criticized

Although the rapper is keen on expanding his family with India, the rapper has been accused of being a deadbeat father by his other baby mamas. 

A woman named Travonna, who alleged that she was the mother of the rapper’s seven-year-old son, claimed that the rapper had ignored their son and failed to support him financially. 

Lil Durk’s baby mama had appeared on blogger Tasha K’s show and called out the rapper for lying about always taking care of his family.

After her comments went live, fans of the rapper lambasted her for being a clout chaser and claimed she was the one lying. 

However, the baby mama responded that she had gotten a DNA test to confirm the rapper's paternity while sharing side-by-side photos of them on Instagram. 

She also added that she could not understand how Royale could shut her eyes to her fiancé’s family mess.
