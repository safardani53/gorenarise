Love Your Garden viewers break down in tears at familys reaction to epic home transformation

 Posted on March 2, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  207 words  |  Aldo Pusey

LOVE Your Garden viewers were reduced to tears while watching a family's reaction to their epic home transformation.

The much-loved ITV format, which is presented by Alan Titchmarsh, aired its latest episode on Tuesday.

It sees deserving people from across the country have their garden and outdoor spaces renovated.

Alan and his team of experts lead the transformations which results in stunning gardens and lifestyle-enhancing.

The next instalment saw Alan and his team travel to Sheffield as they met a new family.

Viewers were introduced to NHS shift workers Seema and Nawras.

The pair both work gruelling hours all the while providing 24-hour care to their disabled daughter.

Telly watchers at home were left moved by the family's story but were reduced to tears by the transformation.

Taking to social media, one viewer said: "To see the smile on the face of that little girl has made me cry #LoveYourGarden."

A second commented: "Her smile such an amazing transformation."
