A TIKTOKER has begged for five fast food items to return as viewers called for another beloved menu choice to be brought back.

Many of the discontinued items on the fast food fans' list were from Taco Bell, a restaurant that seems to have an ever-changing menu.

The first item that TikToker Morgan (@morganchomps) wanted to bring back was Taco Bell's naked chicken chips.

The chicken chips were first introduced in 2017, according to a Taco Bell Wiki fandom page.

It was a side menu item of triangular chicken pieces served with a cup of nacho cheese.

"So, so good and you could get them in a quesadilla," Morgan said in the video from 2022.

Morgan also raved about Taco Bell's discontinued dessert menu.

The Caramel Apple Empanada. "Need I say more?" Morgan asked.

A commenter seconded this by writing in the comment section: "The caramel apple empanadas are fire. Miss them so much!"

"I'll never stop being mad at Taco Bell for the empanada," another TIkToker wrote.

Morgan continued in the video: "Can we just bring back their whole dessert menu?"

"This brownie sandwich was fire," Morgan said pointing to a picture of Taco Bell's Brownie Sandwich.

The Brownie Sandwich consisted of two brownies with a layer of chocolate frosting in the middle.

It seems that others agreed with Morgan on their love for the Caramel Apple Empanada as they have posted copycat recipes online to get their fix of the sweet treat.

'INDESCRIBABLE' FLAVOR

Morgan also brought up Taco Bell's Moutain Dew Spiked Lemonade, which was introduced in 2017, but replaced in 2019 by Moutain Dew Baja Blast Zero Sugar, according to another Wiki fandom page.

"It was so good," Morgan said.

She added: "The flavor paired with Taco Bell's menu items so perfectly - indescribable."

The last Taco Bell item in Morgan's rant was Taco Bell's Grilled Stuft Nacho, which was most recently released in 2015 before being kicked off the menu, according to a WIki fandom page.

This menu item was shaped like a nacho but was big enough to fit beef, cheddar jalapeño sauce, nacho cheese, sour cream, and crunchy red strips in a tortilla.

"The Grilled Stuft Nacho also needs to make a comeback," Morgan said.

"Yes the grilled stuff nacho is my favorite," a TikTok user said in the comment section.

Commenters also took this video as an opportunity to share which menu items they wished would come back.

"Taco Bell's rolled Chicken tacos with cheese dip owned my soul and I could easily eat like four orders of them," a TikToker wrote.

ARBY'S POTATO CAKES

Morgan also brought up a discontinued side dish from Arby's.

"Love or hate Arby's, but their potato cakes dipped in a little bit of Arby's sauce and a little bit of horsey sauce - so good."

"Me and my mom are sick that these are gone."

In May 2021, Arby's confirmed that it would no longer be selling its Potato Cakes.
