Leigh-Anne Pinnock stuns in incredible white wedding dress in first pictures from her exotic wedding

 Posted on May 24, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  306 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

LEIGH-Anne Pinnock has broken her silence on her wedding to Andre Gray and shared a stunning photo album from her big day.

The Little Mix singer confirmed she married footballer Andre in a beach front ceremony in Jamaica, surrounded by friends and family.

The loved-up pop star told fans they got married on June 3.

Leigh-Anne, 31, captioned her photos: "I married my soul mate" alongside a red heart emoji.

Her bandmate Jade Thirlwirll was one of the first to comment on the beautiful snaps.

She penned: "Love you and your beautiful family."

Former Towie star Jess Wright said: "Wow !!!!!! Congratulations beautiful".

And Little Mix dancer Randall Watson said: "And the tears start all over again. Such a beautiful wedding & couple. Let love lead you both always."

Leigh-Anne's photos show the couple tied the knot on the waterfront beneath a flower display in the shape of a palm tree.

The Sweet Melody songstress opted for a princess dress which featured a see-through mesh corset covered in white beads.
