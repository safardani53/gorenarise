How can I buy Joe Pera tour tickets?

 Posted on June 20, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  475 words  |  Martina Birk

COMEDIAN Joe Pera is going on a nationwide tour starting February 22, 2022.

The tour will kick off in New York City before he heads out to the midwest.

Who is Joe Pera Snowman?

Joe Pera stars as a fictionalized version of himself in the show Joe Pera Talks With You on Adult Swim.

The show has wrapped up season three and Pera is now taking his comedic talents to the road as the host in an upcoming tour alongside Saturday Night Live alum and co-producer of Joe Pera Talks with You, Dan Licata.

Pera said he had pitched his short-form TV show to Adult Swim based on his comedic efforts onstage.

He told Deadline in 2020 that he wanted to see how far he could push his low-key brand of comedy and when his pitch for the Joe Pera Talks With You show was accepted, he went into the 4am slot.

"The thing that keeps me calm and steady is being able to keep working on projects and thinking about it," Pera told Deadline.

He added, "I think for all comedians, they just want an audience, and to be able to make work. So, as long as I can work on something, I’ll be happy."

How can I buy tickets to his upcoming tour?

Joe Pera is switching back to the stage for his upcoming Spring in the Midwest and Rustbelt tour.

Fans who want to purchase tickets to his events can visit Pera's website which lists all of his tour dates with the location and venue. The link will redirect buyers to third-party websites where they can purchase the tickets.

The age requirement, cost, and Covid-19 protocols vary depending on the location and those wishing to attend should look up the guidelines prior to purchasing tickets.

Where is Joe Pera going on tour?

Joe Pera will kick off his tour with the Dan Joe DVD Show at The Bell House in Brooklyn, NY on February 22 and March 1. Tickets for this event are already available for purchase through Pera's website.

  • April 30: Two shows at The Comedy Attic in Bloomington Indiana
