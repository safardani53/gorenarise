Pauly D can’t believe he actually found love on Famously Single! And not only did he find it, but he found it with a fellow cast-mate. He explained to the HollywoodLife.com podcast that he and Aubrey’s connection was immediate. “Walking in the house, there were four girls and I could sit at whichever table I wanted to sit at,” Pauly explained. “And I ended up sitting at her table, so I went right over to her. To talk to her.”

He reveals that the real feelings took a little longer to develop. “The connection really happened once we started to get to know each other,” he continued. “The first intros we didn’t really know anyone in the house, I didn’t think I would date anyone right off the bat. And then I developed the connection as I got to know her.” How romantic!

Even though Pauly met tons of women during the show, only one really stood out to him. “We were going out. We were also supposed to participate in these exercises that the therapist gave us to meet other people. We did those, but it got to the point where I was like, damn I don’t even want to do these anymore, I just want to hang out with you,” he reminisced. “Then we saw the therapist. How backwards is that? You’re supposed to meet someone and fall in love, then live together. We lived together immediately, went to therapy immediately.”

Pauly says he’s never had a connection like this before. “I let her in more than I’ve ever let anyone else in before,” he gushed. “As I’m telling her my life story, and she’s listening to my story, I’m just looking at her like, wow, I’m in love with this girl. I’m telling her all this stuff I’ve never told anyone before and she’s loving and appreciating it and doing the same. In that moment where I let her in more than I ever let anyone else into my life, I knew it.” We’re not crying, we just have something in our eye! Listen to the full podcast above!
