Career

Horn claimed the Australian title in 2009 and 2011 as an amateur. Then, in 2012, after having won his third Australian championship and his first Oceania title, he was chosen for the London Olympics. He played a total of three matches at the 2012 Olympics but lost all of them. His first bout was against Gilbert Coombe, his second against Abderrazak Hoya, and his third against Denys Berinchyk.

In March 2013, Jeff Horn won a professional bout against Naoufel Ben Rabah. He defeated Randall Bailey, Rico Mueller, and Ali Funeka in the 2016 match.
