 Posted on May 16, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  528 words  |  Aldo Pusey

DANCING With The Stars fans have begun suspecting that a major pop star's sibling might be using her dancing shoes soon.

The cryptic post shared on the official DWTS Instagram account on Monday had fans guessing which celebrities were going to be the new contestants in the upcoming season.

Three snaps were placed in a carousel post on the dance show's Instagram page.

In each black-and-white image, there was a woman and man posed together in sparkly performance costume ensembles.

No one's face was visible because the dance couples held a disco ball over each other's faces in their respective pics.

"The countdown is ON to find out who this season's #DWTS stars and couples are. Comment with your guesses!," the caption read.

Tons of fans provided their answers in the comment section, responding to the caption.

"I think the first one might be Britt and her new partner!!," one fan replied.

Some fans theorized that one of the contestants was a Boy Meets World alum.

"That second picture looks like Topanga [Danielle Fishel] for sure lol," one fan guessed.

GUESS WHO'S COMING TO DWTS

Countless other people believed that the second slide in the post showed Britney Spears', 41, younger sister Jamie Lynn Spears, 32.

"Second one looks a lot like Jamie Lynn Spears...," one fan noted.

Another person agreed: "2nd pic looks like Jamie Lynn Spears."

A third fan commented: "If that's Alan and Jamie Lynn Spears it's gonna be one roller coaster of a season...."

Someone else declared: "Alan and Jamie Lynn spears."

Though many fans assumed that Jamie was going to appear in the forthcoming DWTS season, there were some fans who weren't convinced.

"Didn't Jamie Lynn Soears already did DWTS? It looks like Danielle Fishel aka Topanga Matthews to me," one fan pointed out.

A second fan admitted: "She was on season 28 in 2019."

Jamie hasn't competed on DWTS yet.

BIG CHANGES

In August, DWTS announced a new season for this fall.

There are many changes coming, including network changes, pro shake-ups, and host drama.

For 32 seasons, DWTS has had numerous celebrities compete with professional dancers to win the mirrorball trophy.

Throughout those seasons, there have been hosts drama, schedule changes, judge switch-ups, and more.

And this season is no exception to that.

Although not everything has been announced for the new season, enough is known for fans to start getting excited.

The first promotional videos were released on August 21, showing what the new season may bring.

Casting rumors have come out, and pros are confirming whether they are returning or not.

At one time, DWTS would air two seasons in one year - one in the spring and one in the fall.

However, in recent years, there has only been one season per year that airs in or around September.
