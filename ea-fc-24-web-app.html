EA FC 24 is just around the corner, and thanks to a clever trick, fans can jump into its most popular mode, Ultimate Team, one or two days before Early Access starts.

Eager football fans will be able to start building their teams earlier through EA FC 24’s dedicated complementary apps that are launching before the main game.

The EA Sports FC Web and Companion apps for EA FC 24 will be released on September 20 and 21, 2023, respectively.

The Web and Companion apps let players manage their Ultimate Team, open UT card packs, and use the transfer market to buy and sell players among other features.

Apart from the features above, the apps also let you solve squad-building challenges (SBCs), claim UT rewards, and even apply contracts and other consumables.

In short, the two apps allow you to do almost everything there is to do in Ultimate Team, except actually playing matches.

The EA Sports FC 24 main game is set to launch into early access on September 22, and the full release is on September 29, 2023.

The game is coming to Windows PCs, PlayStation and Xbox consoles, and the Nintendo Switch.

This means the apps allow you to start building your Ultimate Team more than a week before the main game is out and at least a day before it launches in early access.

The Web and Companion apps are available for free on any web browser and from Apple’s App Store and Google Play.

Using the apps from day one will let you do the legwork by the time the full game is out and then just jump into a match right away when it launches.

To get the app on an Android phone, head to Google Play on Thursday, September 20, search for “EA Sports FC 24 Companion,” and tap “Install”. 

Then just log into the app with the same EA account you have for FIFA 23 or EA FC 24.

The Web app doesn’t ever require installing anything; you just open the EA Sports FC Official Website.

There will be a Web App button to click when the app launches on September 21, and you just log in and start using it.

That’s everything you need to know about EA FC 24’s Web and Companion apps launch and how to use them before the full game is out.
