Who is This Alleged Escort on The Bachelor?

 Posted on September 10, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  535 words  |  Martina Birk

The Bachelor opened on Monday night by talking about a contestant who left the show, Sarah Trott.

But the latest installment of this beloved ABC franchise didn’t waste much time in then shifting its focus to a contestant who just arrived on the show.

Brittany Galvin sure made quite a first impression, didn’t she?

The 23-year old was one of five new suitors to show up on the Pennsylvania set this week, allegedly taking Matt James and his aspiring wives by surprise in the process.

Why did Galvin stand out, however?

Because fellow contestant Anna, who hails from the same city (Chicago) as Brittany, claimed that Galvin works as an escort! For real!

Sounds like a bit of a stretch. How did she attempt to back up this claim?

"She is entertaining men for money," Anna said on air, adding:

"There is a rumor that because she knows all the rich men in Chicago, Brittany may be an escort. She may be having a transactional relationship with wealthy men."

Wow, well… okay then!

Galvin strongly denied all this escort talk when confronted with it by the other contestants.

"It’s just so ridiculous to even say that," she told Anna in front of the group.

"I’ve had a boyfriend my entire life, like since I was 16. Making a conclusion about me before you even know me, that’s s—y.

"I don’t know, I just feel like everyone’s against me right now."

What else do we know about Brittany Galvin? Well…

She is signed to Wilhelmina Models, one of the world’s leading modeling and talent agencies. And, yes, she currently works out of Chicago.

She is seven years younger than lead Matt James.

And she just loves to hang out with people!

“[Galvin] describes herself as a social butterfly that, in a pre-COVID world, loves to go out and have a good time,” her ABC bio reads.

“She dreams of moving to New York City, finding love that broadens her horizons and traveling the world with the man of her dreams.”

Brittany also has said she wants her modeling career to expand to DJing.

As for what she’s looking for in a husband?

“Her ideal man will be fun, mature and trusting. She can’t be with a man who won’t let her live her life, and says she has no time for unnecessary drama," reads the aforementioned bio.

Brittany does Yoga a lot and was a cheerleader in high school.

She enjoys horseback riding and also riding on ATVs.

And, hey, did you know that Brittany’s sister is Missy Galvin? An actress who has appeared in Chicago Fire, Chicago P.D. and Empire, according to IMDb?

It’s true!

“Brittany is a sexy and sassy woman who is ready to take a different approach to her dating life,” her ABC bio also reads.

“Single for the first time in a long time, this serial monogamist is ready to break out of her Chicago bubble and experience love with a new type of man."
