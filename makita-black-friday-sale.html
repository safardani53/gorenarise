    When is Black Friday 2021?

    This year Black Friday lands on November 26. The big day always comes the day after the American holiday Thanksgiving, which is the fourth Thursday of November.

    The Black Friday weekend is known as the biggest shopping period of the year, so make sure to look around this weekend to bag the best deals around.
