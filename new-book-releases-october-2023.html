Spooky and Sweet New Book Releases in October 2023

 Posted on January 30, 2024  |  17 minutes  |  3466 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

These new book releases in October 2023 include several exciting new horror books, romcoms, and spooky historical fiction novels.

There are so many reasons to love October. Between the influx of pumpkin spice products, the chill in the air, and the abundance of spooky décor, it's Halloween all the time for thirty-one days and nights. Regardless of how much you enjoy the festivities, it's also a great month to curl up with new book releases.

As always, Distractify's roundups are non-exhaustive.

'Woke Up Like This' by Amy Lea (Young Adult)

Charlotte Wu has been looking forward to planning her senior prom her entire high school experience — until she falls off a ladder and into the arms of her arch-nemesis, J.T. Renner. When she wakes up, she finds herself as a thirty-year-old, sleeping soundly next to her fiancé ... Renner! Will the duo find their way back to seventeen?

Woke Up Like This releases on Oct. 1, 2023.

Texan Ivy's summer plans are derailed when she finds herself planning a cross-country road trip to get an abortion by staying with her grandmother on the West Coast. Plan A releases on Oct. 3, 2023.
