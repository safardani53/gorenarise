Taylor Swift pays for green card for fan struggling with coronavirus hardships days before deportati

 Posted on January 31, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  504 words  |  Martina Birk

TAYLOR Swift sent $3,000 to pay for the green card for a fan facing deportation after she lost her job due to COVID-19.

The pop superstar, 30, saw a Tumblr post from Skylar Kervin who detailed how she was just days away from being deported back to Canada because she could not afford a new green card.

The Canadian - who now lives in Alabama - exclusively revealed to The Sun: “I was facing deportation.

“My green card renewal was coming up and due to COVID-19, I lost my job. I was stressing about how I was going to afford to renew it.

“I had until Wednesday of next week to get my paperwork sent off. My last day in the US was scheduled for June 22nd,” the Swiftie said of the tight deadline.

Skylar - who has resided in the United States with her husband for five years now - opened up about her recent struggles online and caught the attention of the Lover songwriter.

"I would have been deported had Taylor’s generosity not come through,” the fan confessed.

She continued: “I’m using the money to renew my green card and if there is any remaining afterward I will be using it for a flight home to visit my family who I haven’t seen in over 3 years due to financial struggles.”

Just one day before she received her payment, Skylar opened up about the rush to remain in the United States.

She wrote on Tumblr Wednesday: “At the beginning of March I lost my serving job due to the closing of dine-in service in my state.”

Skylar continued: “I’lI have to renew my green card within the next 2 weeks and I have no idea how I’m now going to afford an extra $680 when I can barely afford to eat and pay my bills.

“Every tear, dollar, piece of paperwork, mile driven to appointments etc. is about to go out the window if they deport me due to the governments strict timelines with things like this.”

Along with a link to her PayPal account, Skylar also revealed that one week after she lost her position, her husband Matt was also out of a job due to the global pandemic.

The following day, Skylar received $3,000 along with a personal message from Taylor.

“I just read about everything you’re having to go through and it was so devastating to hear how much you have on your shoulders.

“I’m so so sorry. You and your family are just trying your best. I hope this gift of $3,000 helps you right now.

“Sending you a huge hug, Taylor,” the Blank Space singer wrote.

The Grammy winner has been sending money to deserving fans all month long as the coronavirus pandemic continues to devastate worldwide.
