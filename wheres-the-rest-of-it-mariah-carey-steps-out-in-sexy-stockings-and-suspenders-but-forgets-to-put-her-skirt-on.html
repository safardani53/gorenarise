EITHER Mariah Carey got dressed in a hurry or she wanted to turn as many heads as possible for her DJing debut.

The singer arrived in Las Vegas last night to take to the decks for the very first time but looked as though she’d forgotten the most vital part of her outfit - her skirt.

Then again, she probably though it didn’t matter what she wore on her bottom half as she’d be hidden by the DJ booth.

As she strutted into the 1 OAK nightclub at the Mirage Hotel & Casino in Sin City, the 46-year-old took a moment to stop and pose up a storm in her very eye-catching outfit.

Maria, 46, certainly looked sexy in a black leather biker jacket, a lacy bodysuit, sheer stockings and suspenders.

Her long blonde hair cascaded down her shoulders in waves and she accessorised with some very sparkling bling around her neck and on her ears as well as her ginormous engagement ring.

She posed in front of the club’s backdrop and kicked up her very high heels to throw some sexy shapes for the camera showing off her recent wright loss and a very toned and pert bottom.

Mariah is currently living in Las Vegas where she is performing a headlining residency at the world-famous Caesar’s Palace.

The show, entitled ‘1 to Infinity’ opened at the four-star hotel and casino in May 2015 and performs on Wednesdays, Saturdays and Sundays only.

Mariah played her last show, which features all the singer's eighteen US number-one hits, for June on Saturday night and then made her way straight to the nightclub for her DJ debut.

And if you thought her outfit for last night’s set was very daring of her, her residency show sees the diamond clad diva going through a number of costume changes.

Mariah got engaged to Australian multi-millionaire businessman James Packer in January and may be able to marry him soon after her divorce from second husband Nick Cannon moved one step closer to being finalised.

The actor, rapper and presenter has finally agreed to sign their divorce papers, after originally filing the documents in December 2014.
