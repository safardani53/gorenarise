While the Love Island contestants are on the search for love, they may sometimes let slip their previous dating history while on the show.

So who is Sommer Ray? What happened between her and the 2020 Islander? Find out below!

What happened between Bennett Sipes and Sommer Ray?

Bennett, who has recently joined the 2020 Love Island series as a new cast member, and model Sommer Ray reportedly lived together as a couple in 2018, but it is alleged that they broke up on bad terms.

It has been alleged that in March 2018, when Sommer and Bennett were still together, that he was involved in a fight with Kourtney Kardashian’s ex Younes Bendjima.
