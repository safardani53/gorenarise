AN Amazon program where people can make nearly $20,000 has been revealed by an enterprising social media sage.

The TikTok influencer said the Amazon Associate marketing program is open to anyone and is perfect if you're "broke or lazy."

Known as makingmoneywithmegan on the platform, Meghan shared how she started her side hustle on Amazon, and you can do it all from home.

What is the Amazon Affiliate Marketing Program?

The Amazon Affiliate marketing program, also known as Amazon Associates, gives an opportunity to website owners to create unique product links, which are also called affiliate links.

The website owner has to promote the link, and once a person visits the link and makes a purchase using the link, Amazon pays a commission to the associate.

How to get started

First, create your account through Amazon's affiliate program site.

How much does Amazon Associates pay?

You can earn typically from $100 to $20,000 for being an Amazon Associate, as HubSpot reported.

It is all dependent on how many people you attracted through your personalized link.

Depending on the product category, some products have a higher commission rate than others.

Some of the product categories that have higher commission rates are Amazon Games and Luxury Beauty.

Amazon Games has a fixed income commission rate of 20 percent and Luxury beauty has 10 percent.

Keep in mind, any extra income needs to be reported to the Internal Revenue Service or you can face hefty penalties come tax time.
