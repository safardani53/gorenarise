Ihop announces menu change with Halloween fan favorite - but there's a catch

 Posted on June 30, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  497 words  |  Martina Birk

IHOP has announced the return of a spooky seasonal favorite.

Halloween is just two weeks away and IHOP is getting in on the festivities.

Starting Tuesday, October 24, the Scary Face Pancakes will be free for kids 12 and under with the purchase of an adult entrée.

These pancakes feature a frightful spin on IHOP's classic Happy Face pancakes, as they're adorned with a whipped cream face, Oreo cookie eyes, a large glazed strawberry nose, and candy corn teeth.

"IHOP has scary face pancakes and I want them. No...NEED them," one fan wrote on X, formerly known as Twitter, when the pancakes launched last year.

This will be the 15th year in a row that the Scary Face pancake returns to menus.

The deal only runs from 4 to 10 pm each day through October 31.

IHOP launched the rest of its fall menu extremely early on August 28.

Diners can enjoy the pumpkin spice pancake combo which comes with two pancakes, made with real pumpkin and seasonal spices, topped with whipped cream, and served with two bacon strips or sausage links, two eggs, and hash browns.

Since IHOP first introduced the pumpkin pancakes in 2008, it has sold an average of over a million each year.

MONSTER MASH

Krispy Kreme is also serving up some scary good treats for Halloween.

The doughnut company teamed up with the Scooby-Doo franchise to launch a collection of spooky treats.

Dave Skena, Global Chief Brand Officer for Krispy Kreme released a statement saying: "We’ve asked Scooby-Doo and his friends to solve ‘The Case of the Disappearing Doughnuts’ because it seems like whenever we put out a dozen Krispy Kreme doughnuts, they vanish almost immediately.

"Scooby-Doo is the perfect franchise this Halloween season for a fun collection of all-new doughnuts and to solve this spooky mystery."

Customers can order a Scooby Dooby-Doo doughnut, Spooky Monster Cookies N' Kreme doughnut, Jinkies! doughnut, and Mystery Machine doughnut.

They all start with a classic Original Glazed doughnut, except for the Spooky Monster one, which is a filled doughnut.

The Scooby Dooby-Doo doughnut features blue icing, a Scooby-Doo dog decoration, lime green icing swirls, and orange cake batter buttercream in the center.

The Mystery Machine doughnut has chocolate icing with chocolate sprinkles, a Mystery Machine decoration, and lime green icing.

The Jinkies! doughnut is topped with orange icing and festive sprinkles.

Finally, the Spooky Monster Cookies n' Kreme doughnut is topped with purple icing, Oreo pieces, cookies and creme filling, a drizzle of black chocolate icing, and a decoration of one of three classic Scooby-Doo monsters; that The Creeper, The Space Kook, and The Yeti.
