Ashley Graham Looks Unrecognizable After Weight Loss Journey

 Posted on April 4, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  673 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Ashley Graham has always been a role model to plus-size women worldwide.

She has promoted being confident in one’s body without the societal pressure of being skinny. Similarly, she often posts raw photos on social media so that her fans and followers realize that nobody is perfect.

But recently, netizens have noticed that Graham has lost significant weight. She looks different from before looking at the recent photos on the supermodel’s Instagram.

Therefore, people have claimed that Graham must have gone through an incredible weight loss journey. Moreover, they believe that Graham no longer fits in the category of “plus-size.”

Did Ashley Graham lose weight?

At the Vanity Fair x Prada party at the Cannes Film Festival, Graham shimmered in a seductive, translucent ensemble, showing a leaner figure. She looked stunning as she attended the upscale party wearing a glittery see-through Prada dress with a black bra and underwear. 

With her hair in a bouncy blowout, she accessorized the silver and black dress with a matching jewel-encrusted Prada purse, metallic heels, and shoulder-grazing silver earrings. Graham looked exquisite as always, but people pointed out that Graham must have gone through weight loss.

She looked more toned than before, and her jawline was “snatched.” Netizens took to the comment section to voice out their opinion on how the supermodel looked unrecognizable.

They also noted that she could no longer be considered plus-size. One fan wrote, “Not a plus size model anymore. You look amazing like this as well.” “Her face looks so different pretty but not the same as it did before so sad,” another penned.

Similarly, another person commented, “Has anyone noticed Ashley is getting more trimmer???” “Beautiful either way but who are the (real) “plus size” models now?” a netizen wrote.

Netizen’s reaction to Ashley Graham’s weight loss journey. (Source: Instagram)

Some netizens mourned that they lost another plus-size model to weight loss. “I didn’t even recognize who this was, another curvy and perfect celeb lost to the Ozempic craze,” one penned.

However, there are many people who defended Graham, her weight loss, and her current physique. They noted that it was not up to her fans and followers to dictate her lifestyle.

Furthermore, they noted that she had three kids and weight loss was involuntary when one’s body underwent many changes after giving birth. One fan wrote, “She is gorgeous! Period! And she is a mother of 3 and we don’t know nothing about her reasons of losing weight if she did and it’s not our business!!”

Similarly, another commented, “why people are so negative and critical, only to say negative things. She’s amazing!! you only think she doesn’t look like a plus-size model because she normalized plus-size modeling. good energy and be happy.” 

All in all, netizens have mixed feelings about Graham’s weight loss journey.

How did Ashley Graham achieve weight loss?

Graham maintains a balanced diet and workouts quite often. While she does this to keep her body healthy and toned, perhaps, this is one of the reasons behind her weight loss journey.

The supermodel told Stylecraze that she mostly eats fresh fruits, vegetables, lean protein, and healthy fats. For breakfast, she usually has a smoothie made of ginger, kale, apple, lemon, parsley, and beetroot.

She has Quinoa and brown rice bowl with veggies, topped with tahini for lunch and Baked salmon, arugula, and sweet potato for dinner. Similarly, for snacks, she eats Chocolate Tree chia crackers.

During her cheat day, she has mac and cheese for either lunch or dinner. Alongside a healthy diet, Graham’s weight loss journey is aided by her workouts.

She frequently posts images of herself working out on her Instagram and other social media platforms. She works out with Dawin Pena and does strength training exercises, including kickboxing, sledgehammer swings, planks, push-ups, barbell squats, and resistance band hip thrusts.

She also does sumo deadlifts as well as HIIT exercises like jumping squats, lunges, burpees, ball smash, scissor legs, and jackknifes. She often runs and participates in aerial stretching sessions to maintain her flexibility.
